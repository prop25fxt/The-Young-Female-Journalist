About Young Female Journalists Network

Young Female Journalists Network (YFJN) is a Nigeria‑based organization headquartered in Abuja, dedicated to correcting gender disparity in our media industry by transforming ambitious students into high‑impact professionals.

Who we are

In Nigeria’s evolving media landscape, the story is often told — but the storytellers remain unbalanced. YFJN exists to ensure the transition from student to professional is not where brilliant female voices are lost.

We bridge the gap. We build the bridge.

Vision

A future where young women journalists are visible, skilled, safe, and respected — shaping narratives locally and globally.

Mission

To cultivate a new vanguard of female journalists equipped with strategic foresight, ethical grounding, and technical mastery required to lead national discourse and contribute to sustainable nation‑building.

Core values

Integrity

Ethical reporting, truth‑seeking, and accountability.

Excellence

Continuous improvement in craft and leadership.

Inclusion & Safety

Equity, dignity, and protection for women in media spaces.
