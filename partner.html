Partner with us

Young Female Journalists Network is more than a mentorship group — it is a strategic solution to gender imbalance in Nigeria’s media and leadership sectors. We invite media houses, NGOs, and corporate stakeholders to partner with us in institutionalizing gender equity.

Why partner

  • Talent pipeline: access a vetted pool of newsroom‑ready female journalists
  • CSR & impact: align with SDGs (Goal 5: Gender Equality; Goal 8: Decent Work)
  • National reach: support high‑visibility projects like Land Care and youth‑led radio broadcasts
