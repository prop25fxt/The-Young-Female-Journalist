Programs

Our programs translate mentorship and ethics into practical newsroom readiness — from classroom learning to real assignments, publication, and leadership.

Strategic program pillars

Industry Navigation & Career Readiness

Blueprints for resilience, career planning, and opportunity discovery in an evolving media landscape.

Ethos‑Driven Mentorship

Guidance from seasoned veterans focused on integrity, civic responsibility, and professional standards.

Hands‑On Assignments & Publication

Beyond theory: field reporting, broadcast practice, and an editorial pipeline that builds portfolios.

Gender Parity Advocacy

Visibility, capability, and leadership — restructuring newsroom representation through talent development.

Flagship initiative: Land Care

A student‑produced radio programme and environmental reporting project designed to build real broadcast capacity and advocacy skills.
